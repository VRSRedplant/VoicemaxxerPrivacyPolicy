Privacy Policy

VoiceMaxxer
Effective Date: January 17, 2026

VoiceMaxxer (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the VoiceMaxxer mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

By using VoiceMaxxer, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the Service.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name (if provided), and authentication data when you create an account or sign in.
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings you create during practice sessions.
  • Support Communications: Information you provide if you contact support.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App interactions, session timestamps, feature usage, and crash reports.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and anonymized identifiers.
  • Approximate Location: Country or region inferred from IP address for legal compliance and service optimization.

1.3 Payment Information

Payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not store your full payment card details. We receive limited transaction information such as subscription status and product identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the Service
  • Store and display your practice history and progress
  • Generate structured feedback and voice analysis
  • Manage subscriptions and access levels
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Communicate with you about service updates or support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Voice Data & Audio Privacy

Your voice recordings are treated as private content.

  • Audio recordings are stored securely and linked only to your account
  • You may delete your recordings at any time within the app
  • If auto-delete is enabled, recordings are removed after the selected period
  • We do not sell or share your voice data with third parties

Voice recordings may be temporarily processed by trusted third-party services to provide transcription and feedback generation. These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only to deliver the requested service.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the UK or European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide the Service you request
  • Consent: For optional features such as notifications or feedback processing
  • Legitimate interests: To improve and secure the Service
  • Legal obligations: To comply with applicable laws

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account data is stored while your account remains active
  • Audio recordings are retained based on your app settings
  • Usage and diagnostic data may be retained for system improvement and legal compliance

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, transcription, analytics, and feedback generation services
  • Payment Platforms: Subscription verification via Apple or Google
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights

All service providers are required to protect your data and use it only to provide their services.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information, including standard contractual clauses where required.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

9. Children’s Privacy

VoiceMaxxer is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Third-Party Services

The Service may use third-party tools for authentication, payments, speech transcription, feedback generation, and analytics. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website with an updated effective date.
