Common Questions

I paid but Pro isn’t unlocked.

  • Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used to purchase.
  • Open the app → Settings → tap Restore Purchases.
  • If it still doesn’t unlock, email support with your purchase date.

How do I cancel a subscription?

  • On iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → VoiceMaxxer → Cancel.
  • Apple manages billing and cancellations directly.

Microphone access isn’t working.

  • iPhone Settings → VoiceMaxxer → enable Microphone.
  • Close and reopen the app after changing permissions.

How is my voice data handled?

  • Your recordings are private and used only for analysis and feedback.
  • You can delete recordings in-app at any time.
  • Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting Checklist

  • Update to the latest app version.
  • Restart your phone.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • If the issue persists, send us a message with the details below.

When contacting support, please include:

• Device model (e.g., iPhone 16 Pro)
• iOS version
• App version
• What you were doing when the issue happened
• Any screenshots or screen recordings (optional)

VoiceMaxxer is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not sell personal data.
